FAQ

What is SPS?

The SPS is a smart garage automation system that opens and closes automatically, detects your vehicle, and ensures safety and convenience through intelligent sensors.

Which sensors are used in the system?

We use ultrasonic,temperature, capacity sensors to detect approaching vehicles, an infrared sensor to detect parking position, and a tactile switch or graphical user interface to trigger garage closure.

How does the system know the garage is occupied?

A capacity sensor detects if a vehicle is inside. The status is shown on a display and a red/green LED indicator.

Is the lighting automatic?

Yes, once the vehicle is parked and detected, the garage light turns red and switches to green after leaving the garage.

Where can I buy the components?
